#TreeView.

TreeView is AngularJs application, which allows you to make your own recursive tree view.

Functionality:
1. For making your tree rolling, press "Get it started!" button. It will execute your tree from scratch.
2. For adding new node, press "+" button near the current one.
3. For removing node, press trash button near your node.
4. For saving you tree to the storage, press "Save tree" button.
5. There is "Restore tree" button for restoring your tree from the storage.
6. For executing all tests, press "Execute tests" button.
